WHAT THE PROGRAM DOES BAIXEASSISTA.
==========================
- Removes the time limit imposed by the video servers online.
- Download and displays in the internal or external player, the video file.
- Displays the video file straight from the program.
- Resume previously stopped downloads.
- Generate urls history of downloaded video.
- Speeds up the download, because several connections can be created at the same time.
- And much more ...

PLAYER VIDEO
==========================
- BaixeAssista The program offers two options player:
- The embedded (jwplayer - widely used on the Internet).
-- And the external (as SPlayer; FLV player and some others), must be downloaded and installed separately.

BROWSER
==========================
- Server-based to program. He is the source urls.
- With the browser, you can extract the videos of urs due to "thumbnail" or automatic capture.
- With a simple click on the "thumbnail", the url is captured and inserted the correct control.
- Clicking the "Download", the download starts immediately.
** Tip! - You can download videos without using the browser. Just copy and paste the url.
** Tip! - It is important to know if the program supports the url given to it, see the list below.

SERVERS PROXIES
==========================
- Since the goal is to make the program to the anonymous world of the internet in relationship the
--transfer of videos several different ips are used.
- To make easier the use of proxy servers, a researcher is available ips.
- Once configured properly, it does its job and generates a new list of ips,
with servers active and ready to transfer video files.
